## Main version (~1,200 characters)

I shipped my new portfolio — this time I optimized for **how recruiters actually browse** when they have 90 seconds between meetings.

The goal: **tell my career as a journey**, not a static job list.

What I built:

→ **Interactive timeline** — each milestone is a role with period, context, and tech stack (full-time, contractor, personal ventures)

→ **3D particle planet** (Three.js) — the hero silhouette dissolves and reshapes as you scroll through sections. Visual with purpose, not just decoration

→ **Smart PDF resume** — generates a tailored PDF in the browser by role focus (Backend, Tech Lead, AI/LLM…) and tech filters. One click, custom version

→ **PT / EN + light/dark theme** — one site for local and international opportunities

→ **Mãos Livres highlight** (maoslivres.com) — my automation & custom software company, visible upfront

Detail I'm proud of: **I removed forced scroll** on the timeline. The old animation locked visitors in; now recruiters move at their own pace. UX > gimmicks.

Stack: Next.js 16 · React 19 · TypeScript · Tailwind · Framer Motion · Three.js · @react-pdf/renderer

This was a project where **engineering and professional storytelling** go together — production-grade code, content I'd send in a real hiring process.
